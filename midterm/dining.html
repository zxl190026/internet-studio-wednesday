Dining Among the Ocean

Veranda Aperitivo Bar

Veranda Aperitivo Bar settled at the top of the Caldera Cliff on a private terrace of the Canaves Oia Sunday Suites, in the heart of Oia village. The restaurant is easily accessible from all alleys, and it is near all the public parking areas. The menu is a fusion of Japanese and Greek culinary traditions, with each dish crafted to order.

Pyrgos Restaurant

Pyrgos Restaurant is located in the very beautiful village of Pyrgos in one of the highest points of Santorini offering stunning views of the island! The fancy restaurant owns five different halls, indoor and outdoor areas, modern facilities, and elegant décor that make it ideal for any kind of event. Its history dates back to 1980 and today it is one of the best and most successful wedding and event venues on the island.

Aegean Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Santorini, in the village of Imerovigli, near the historic Skaros Castle, Aegean Restaurant is a rustic culinary treasure waiting to be uncovered. This restaurant is situated near the main square, offering one of the island's most privileged locations right along the caldera's embrace. "Aegean" is a charming establishment that seamlessly blends simplicity with strong traditional influences.

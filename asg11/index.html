Dodgeball All-Stars

Velocity Vanguards

We’re a fast, agile, and slightly chaotic dodgeball team made of heroes and legends from different worlds. Every member was picked for speed, reflex, or just pure intimidation. Let’s meet the squad.

Evasion

Spider-Man

Superhuman reflexes and spider-sense make him almost impossible to hit — perfect for dodging fast throws.

Strategy

BatMan

Tactical, flexible, and great at reading opponents. He can call plays and coordinate surprise throws.

Agility

Ant-Man

With his ability to shrink, grow, and move unpredictably, Ant-Man can dodge almost any throw and sneak in surprise shots.

Power

Wonder Woman

Strong throws, great blocking, and she brings team morale up. Also, nobody wants to get on her bad side.

Speed

Deadpool

Deadpool is good at breaking the rules and taking unexpected actions, making it difficult for opponents to predict his next step.

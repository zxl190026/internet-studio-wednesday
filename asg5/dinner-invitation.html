Dinner Invitation

I've invited three special guests—Robert Oppenheimer, Albert Einstein, and Elon Musk—to discuss science, ethics, and the future.

Guests

J. Robert Oppenheimer was a theoretical physicist who coordinated the Manhattan Project at Los Alamos. I invited him because he offers a unique and insightful perspective on how technology is changing the world and the ethical boundaries of scientists.

Einstein, who developed the theory of relativity, is one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century. I invited him because he represents the imagination of physics and often reminds us to examine the significance of science with humanistic care.

Elon Musk is an entrepreneur who is driving the adoption of commercial spaceflight and electric vehicles. I invited him to the table hoping to connect the perspectives of "science and industry implementation" with the thoughts of two theoretical giants.
